const Complex dbs::Span::at ( float  pos) const

Find point on the arc with more uniform distribution (compared to [])

Parameters
posfloat
Returns
const Complex

Maps -1 to arc's begin, 0 to middle of arc, +1 to ark's end.

Other points from [-1, +1] are mapped to arc's points.

Resulting distribution is as much uniform as possible, whereas []'s points tend to concentrate near arc's ends (for big bulge values).
